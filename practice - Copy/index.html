Redefining Premium Living
in Katraj

Kakkad Ambiience Vivanta, Katraj is a premium residential project by Kakkad Group & Ambiience Group, located near ISKCON Temple, Katraj–Kondhwa Road, South Pune. It offers 2 & 3 BHK luxury apartments with the option of Jodi flats, blending modern architecture with spiritual surroundings. This GEM Platinum-rated project comprises 2 towers of G + 18 storeys, featuring thoughtfully designed homes that ensure comfort, elegance, and functionality for every family.
